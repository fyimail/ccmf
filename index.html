CCMF Upcoming Plans

2017-2018

  • New Drug Testing

    A new drug to be tested in-vitro and in-vivo targeting Serotonin receptor 2 in collaboration with Dana Farber Cancer Centre in Boston

  • Ongoing Drug Trials

    Ongoing acceptance of children across 9 treatment sites in Canada for TOPAZ drug trial

  • New Therapies

    Potential Project: To identify and test novel therapies focusing on drug targets of metastasis using neuroblastoma mouse models

  • New Gene Modeling

    Potential Project: Modeling new genes identified in patients with relapsed neuroblastoma to see which ones are causative and/or if they can predict sensitivity to therapies (old and new)
